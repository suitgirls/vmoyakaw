Valentina Oyakawa's Biography

Valentina Oyakawa is a girl that was born in Lima, Peru on the 30th October. Currently, she is 17 years old and she has a younger sister called Daniela. Valentina wants to study civil or aerospace engineering because honestly she deeply likes to build things that have a big impact.Valentina has finished school in 2024 in Peru also and right now she is taking some civil engineering courses back in her country and preparing for the college applications.

Valentina joined the Empowering Women in Technology program to deepen her knowledge in technology and use it to create a positive impact in the world. She is especially interested in both antisismic construction technology and cybersecurity. Valentina also hopes to inspire and encourage more women to pursue careers in tech.

Outside of school, Valentina enjoys working out, watching movies, baking pastries, and most of all spending time with her loved ones. She comes from a close-knit family, even though her parents are divorced. A fun fact about her: if you're friends with her on TikTok, expect to receive around 100 videos a day,no joke! Valentina believes everyone has a purpose tied to the well-being of others, and one of her future goals is to create an NGO that builds eco-friendly, low-cost housing for those in need. She's known for her sense of humor and being a great friend, always open to random conversations and new connections.

Personal Information

Valentina believes deeply in fairness, inclusion, and respect for every person's potential. She values honesty and always acts with integrity, standing up for what is right even when it is difficult. Empowering women is important to her because she believes everyone deserves equal opportunities to grow and lead. She cares about supporting others with kindness and building a world where women feel confident to use their voices and talents in technology and beyond.
